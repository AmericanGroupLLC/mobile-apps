Card — instant action capture across all your devices.

This release ships the brutally simple v1:

  • One feed of Cards on iPhone, Apple Watch, Android, Wear OS
  • One-tap convert: Note → Task → Reminder
  • Real OS-scheduled reminders (Notification Center / AlarmManager)
  • Four quick-capture surfaces:
      – iOS Share Extension
      – Apple Watch "Quick capture" complication
      – Android Quick Settings tile
      – Wear OS tile

No account. No cloud sync. No tracking by default.
Crash reporting + analytics are opt-in from Settings.
