Pocket — five Apple-style native utilities in one app.

This release combines five hand-built tools across iPhone, Apple Watch,
Android, and Wear OS:

  • Clock — alarms, world clock, stopwatch, timer, bedtime
  • Calculator — basic + scientific (rotate to landscape)
  • Measure — ARKit / ARCore, with on-screen ruler fallback
  • Compass — magnetic + true heading, lat/lon, accuracy
  • Level — flat-surface bullseye + tilted-surface bubble

No account. No tracking by default. Crash reporting opt-in.
