Meet people at your pace.

Layered dating in your orbit. Choose Server, State, County, or ZIP discovery. Public-meetup framing. Verification before chat. iPhone, Apple Watch, Android, Wear OS.

WHY DRIFT
- Privacy first - no account required, no tracking by default.
- Native on every platform - SwiftUI for Apple, Jetpack Compose for Android.
- Open source under MIT - audit anything; build it yourself if you don't trust us.

WHATS INCLUDED
- Full Drift experience on iPhone and Apple Watch.
- Identical feature set on Android and Wear OS.
- Localised UI strings; more languages coming.
- Optional opt-in crash reporting (Sentry) and product analytics (PostHog) - both off by default.

GET INVOLVED
Issues, ideas and pull requests at https://github.com/AmericanGroupLLC/DriftDate - we read every one.
