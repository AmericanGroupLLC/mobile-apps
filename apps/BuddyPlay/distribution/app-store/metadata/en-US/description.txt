Local multiplayer. No internet.

Two phones nearby - by Wi-Fi, Hotspot, or Bluetooth - pair and play. No accounts. No servers. No tracking.

WHY BUDDYPLAY
- Privacy first - no account required, no tracking by default.
- Native on every platform - SwiftUI for Apple, Jetpack Compose for Android.
- Open source under MIT - audit anything; build it yourself if you don't trust us.

WHATS INCLUDED
- Full BuddyPlay experience on iPhone.
- Identical feature set on Android.
- Localised UI strings; more languages coming.
- Optional opt-in crash reporting (Sentry) and product analytics (PostHog) - both off by default.

Issues, ideas and pull requests at https://github.com/AmericanGroupLLC/BuddyPlay - we read every one.
