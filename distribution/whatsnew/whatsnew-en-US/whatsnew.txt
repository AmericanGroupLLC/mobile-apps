MyHealth — your personal fitness OS.

What's new in this release:
- Guest Mode: use the app with no email, no account.
- Medicine reminders with Take / Snooze 10 min actions.
- Vitals dashboard with biological-age estimation.
- Anatomy picker: tap a muscle, find exercises with form tips.
- Bundled health articles for offline reading.
- Compose Material 3 UI with dynamic theming and dark mode.
- 5 starter languages: en, es, fr, de, hi.

Privacy: everything stays on your device by default. Cloud sync is optional and only on if you sign in.
