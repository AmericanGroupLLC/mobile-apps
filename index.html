The five tools

⏰ Clock

Real OS-scheduled alarms, world clock, stopwatch, timer, bedtime.

🔢 Calculator

Basic in portrait. Scientific (sin, cos, log, π, x²) in landscape.

📏 Measure

Two taps to size anything. ARKit on iPhone, ARCore on Android. On-screen ruler fallback.

🧭 Compass

Magnetic + true heading, cardinal label, lat/lon panel, accuracy ring.

📐 Level

Bullseye when flat. Single-axis bubble when tilted. Long-press to calibrate.

Private by design

  • No account. No login.
  • Calculator history, headings, AR measurements, alarm names — all stay on device.
  • Crash reporting + product analytics are off by default; opt in from Settings.
  • Camera (Measure) + Location (Compass true heading) only used when their tool is open.
