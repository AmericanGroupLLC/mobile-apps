How "offline" actually works

  1. One-time model download. On first launch the app downloads a ~1 GB language model file over Wi-Fi. The Consent screen tells you the size + Wi-Fi requirement before the download starts.
  2. SHA-256 verified. If the download is corrupt, we re-download. If a CDN goes down, we try a mirror.
  3. That's it. Toggle airplane mode after first launch and every feature still works. The app never reaches the internet for chat, voice, or keyboard.

The only network traffic after first launch is RevenueCat's receipt validation when you subscribe (and the optional AdMob interstitial for ad-unlocks on the free tier).

Privacy by absence

No accounts. No servers we run. No analytics by default. No chat content ever leaves your phone. Offline AI Buddy literally cannot share your conversations because it doesn't have anywhere to send them.

Profiles, chat history, and quota counters live in plain JSON files on your phone. Settings → Erase any time. Done.
